# Cookie Policy

**Last Updated: April 12, 2026**

This Cookie Policy explains how Aithra-NEXUS uses cookies and similar technologies to recognize you when you visit our portal.

## 1. What are cookies?
Cookies are small data files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website.

## 2. Why do we use cookies?
We use cookies for:
- **Essential Cookies**: Necessary for the portal to function (e.g., maintaining your login session).
- **Preference Cookies**: To remember your settings (e.g., theme preferences).
- **Analytics Cookies**: To understand how users interact with the portal (optional).

## 3. Cookie Consent
Upon your first visit to our portal, you will be Map presented with a consent banner. You can choose to accept or decline non-essential cookies.

## 4. Managing Cookies
You can set or amend your web browser controls to accept or refuse cookies. If you choose to reject cookies, you may still use our website though your access to some functionality and areas of our website may be restricted.
