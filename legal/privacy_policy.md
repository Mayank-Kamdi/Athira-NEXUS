# Privacy Policy for Aithra-NEXUS

**Effective Date: April 12, 2026**

Welcome to Aithra-NEXUS, a local-first agentic knowledge vault. Your privacy is our top priority. Because Aithra-NEXUS is designed as a secure, local-first application, we take extreme measures to ensure your data stays under your control.

## 1. Information We Collect
- **Account Information**: If you use our cloud sync or portal features, we collect your email address and username for authentication purposes.
- **Usage Data**: We may collect anonymized telemetry regarding application performance and feature usage to improve the platform.
- **Vault Data**: **IMPORTANT**: Your vault content (nodes, edges, insights) is stored locally on your device and encrypted. We do NOT have access to your decryption keys or your raw vault data unless you explicitly use a cloud-sync feature (where data remains end-to-end encrypted).

## 2. How We Use Information
- To provide and maintain the Service.
- To manage your Account.
- To provide customer support.
- To gather analysis or valuable information so that we can improve our Service.

## 3. Data Security
We use industry-standard AES-256 encryption for your local data. For cloud features, we utilize secure authentication providers (Firebase).

## 4. Your Rights (GDPR/CCPA)
You have the right to access, update, or delete the information we have on you. Since most data is local, you can delete your vault at any time by removing the local database file.

## 5. Contact Us
For any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at: support@aithra-nexus.io
